Land Registration

📝

Our digital land registration service allows you to register your property without the hassle of visiting multiple government offices. The entire process is handled online, from document submission to verification and approval.

Features:

  • Complete digital submission of all required documents
  • Secure online verification of property details
  • Automated checks for document authenticity
  • Digital signature verification
  • Real-time status updates via SMS and email
  • Digitally signed certificates issued directly to your account

With our state-of-the-art digital infrastructure, what used to take weeks or months can now be completed in just a few days, saving you time, money, and unnecessary stress.

Start Registration

Property Records

🔍

Access comprehensive property records from our centralized database. Whether you're a buyer, seller, or just researching, our property records service provides detailed and accurate information about land properties.

Available Information:

  • Current and historical ownership details
  • Property boundaries and survey information
  • Transaction history and property transfers
  • Legal encumbrances and mortgages
  • Tax payment history
  • Land use and zoning information

Our property records are updated in real-time, ensuring you always have access to the most current information. Search by property ID, address, owner name, or survey number.

Search Records

Property Valuation

📊

Our AI-powered property valuation tool provides an accurate estimate of your property's market value based on various factors including location, size, amenities, and current market trends.

Valuation Factors:

  • Location and neighborhood analysis
  • Property size, dimensions, and frontage
  • Recent sales of comparable properties
  • Development potential and zoning regulations
  • Market trends and economic factors
  • Infrastructure and amenities in the vicinity

Our valuation reports are recognized by banks and financial institutions for loan purposes. Get detailed insights into your property's worth with our comprehensive valuation service.

Get Valuation

Document Verification

📄

Ensure the authenticity and validity of property documents with our document verification service. We check for legal compliance, authenticity, and any potential issues that could affect property transactions.

Verification Checks:

  • Authenticity of property titles and deeds
  • Legal validity of documents
  • Encumbrance checks for loans or legal disputes
  • Property tax payment status
  • Building permit and approval verification
  • Signature and stamp verification

Our document verification service helps prevent fraud and ensures that your property transaction proceeds smoothly without any legal complications in the future.

Verify Documents

Fee Payment

💸

Our secure online payment system allows you to pay all registration fees, stamp duties, and other charges directly through our portal. No more standing in queues or dealing with cash payments.

Payment Features:

  • Automatic fee calculation based on property details
  • Multiple payment options (credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI)
  • Secure payment gateways with encryption
  • Instant digital receipts
  • Payment history tracking
  • GST invoicing for applicable services

All transactions are securely processed and immediately reflected in your application status. The system automatically applies the correct fee structures based on current government regulations.

Make Payment

Track Applications

📱

Stay informed about the status of your registration application with our real-time tracking system. Receive notifications at every stage of the process and know exactly when your application is approved.

Tracking Features:

  • Real-time status updates of your application
  • SMS and email notifications for status changes
  • Detailed timeline of application progress
  • Instant alerts for any required clarifications
  • Document verification status tracking
  • Certificate issuance notification

Our application tracking system provides complete transparency throughout the registration process. You can also contact our support team directly through the portal if you have any questions.
