Registration Process

Complete your land registration in a few simple steps

1

Create an Account

Sign up with your email and verify your identity using Aadhaar/PAN card to get started.

2

Fill Property Details

Enter all required property details including location, boundaries, area, and ownership information.

3

Upload Documents

Upload necessary documents like sale deed, title deed, survey documents, and identity proofs.

4

Pay Fees

Calculate and pay registration fees, stamp duty, and other applicable charges securely online.

5

Verification

Your application will be verified by authorities. You'll be notified if any clarification is needed.

6

Receive Certificate

Once approved, download your digitally signed registration certificate and property documents.
