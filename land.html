Video Recording Process

Simple steps to record your land transaction

How to Record Your Transaction

  1. Open our mobile app and navigate to "New Transaction"
  2. Allow camera and location permissions when prompted
  3. Capture the land boundaries by walking the perimeter
  4. Record verbal agreements with all involved parties
  5. Document any payment transactions
  6. Verify the recording using biometric authentication
  7. Upload directly to our secure blockchain storage
